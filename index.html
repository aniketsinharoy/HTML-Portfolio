Lake Umiam, nestled in Meghalaya's verdant bosom, reflects nature's artistry with azure waters mirroring lush hills. Dawki River, a crystalline marvel in Meghalaya, meanders like a silver ribbon amidst emerald landscapes. Its glass-like waters reflect nature's splendor, while the living roots bridge and vibrant cultures unite, making it a timeless testament to the enchanting harmony between man and nature.
